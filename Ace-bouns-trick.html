trick 6

Ace trick

For this trick you will need: a single deck of cards

The set up is very simple, simply put all for aces on top. Now split the deck into 4 different piles with the top pile on your right. Pick up the first pile on the left and put 3 cards on the bottom and add 3 cards to the other piles (A total of 6 cards moved from the top of that pile) Do this for all of the piles, now ask your spectator 'How many cards are there in a standard deck?' they should say '52' but now you need to say 'and how many aces?'they should say 4 now flip over the top card on each pile. when a 4 aces are on top they will be AMAZED
