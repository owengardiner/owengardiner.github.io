The Flipped Card

This trick needs very little set up but it does need very good presenting. for it to work.

For this trick you will need: A deck of cards. The set up is very simple, simply flip the bottom card. Now the trick is very simple. Fan out the cards so that the person can not see that the bottom card is flipped. They pick a card and as they are looking at there card, do not flip the bottom card only the deck they then slide there card into the deck. Now tell them to imagine there card and now you flip over he bottom card, now spread the deck out there card is the one that is flipped over

This trick is good for pulling someone aside and asking if they like magic, not the best for stage acts. And you know PRACTICE
